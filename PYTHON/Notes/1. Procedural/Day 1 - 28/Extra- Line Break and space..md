

## \n: 

`\n` is used to break a line: 
#### Example:
```python
print('i am a boy\nWho is a good one')

# Output:
i am a boy
Who is a good one
```

## \s:

`\s` is same as using space(' ') in a string. 

#### Example:
```python
print('i am a boy\sWho is a good one')

# Output:
i am a boy Who is a good one
```
